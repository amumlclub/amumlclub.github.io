About Us

The Machine Learning Club at Aligarh Muslim University is a student-driven community exploring artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data science.

Through workshops, collaborative projects, research initiatives, and hackathons, we aim to bridge theoretical learning with practical innovation.

Our mission is to create a space where students learn, build, and push the boundaries of intelligent systems together.
